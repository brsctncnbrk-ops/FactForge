## Hook Promise Audit

Title Promise:
Why Rome Was Already Dead Before It Fell

Thumbnail Promise:
ALREADY DEAD / NOBODY NOTICED

Hook Match:
PASS

Reason:
The first 20 seconds (Scenes 1–4) establish all three promised points: the fall
of 476 that almost nobody noticed (Scenes 1–2), the empire already dying for
three hundred years before it (Scene 3), and the correction of the popular
barbarian-invasion memory (Scene 4). The internal-financial killer promised by
the packaging is set up immediately after, in the Problem Setup (Scenes 6–10).
No misleading angle, no generic intro, cold open on 476.
