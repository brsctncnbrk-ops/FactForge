## Hook Promise Audit

Title Promise:
Why the 2008 Financial Crisis Really Happened

Thumbnail Promise:
A falling stock ticker / crumbling bank tower (thumbnail concept 1, primary pairing) — WHAT IT REALLY COST

Hook Match:
PASS

Reason:
The first 20 seconds (Scenes 1-2, through 15.2s) open with Lehman Brothers'
concrete, dramatic collapse and land the $639B/largest-bankruptcy fact,
directly matching the crumbling-bank-tower thumbnail concept; Scene 3 (15.2s)
immediately pivots to the "why" promise ("you have to go back further"),
delivering the title's causal-explanation promise within the 20-second
window. Thumbnail concept 3 (the TARP price-tag reveal) is intentionally
NOT the paired main-video thumbnail — see the note in 05A — since that
reveal doesn't land until Scene 10 (~65s in); pairing it with the main
thumbnail would over-promise against the first 20 seconds.
